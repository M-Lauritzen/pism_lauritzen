.. _sec-citing-pism:

Citing PISM
===========

There are many use cases for citing software, including referring to the software used in
producing the results of a study and discussing modeling approaches (continuum models and
their discretizations, parameterizations, algorithms, or approximations) provided by a
piece of software :cite:`Katz2021`.

If you used PISM or PISM outputs from an earlier study directly in the research described
in your article we recommend citing the specific version of PISM you used. Please see
:ref:`sec-publishing` for more recommendations.

When discussing PISM more broadly, we recommend citing PISM as a project.

In addition to citing a specific PISM version or PISM as a project, please cite one or
both of the following references, as appropriate.

.. code-block:: bibtex
   :name: pism-citations
   :caption: Recommended PISM citations

   @article {BuelerBrown2009,
     AUTHOR = {E. Bueler and J. Brown},
      TITLE = {Shallow shelf approximation as a "sliding law" in a
               thermodynamically coupled ice sheet model},
    JOURNAL = {J. Geophys. Res.},
     VOLUME = {114},
        DOI = {10.1029/2008JF001179},
       YEAR = {2009},
   }

   @article {Winkelmannetal2011,
     AUTHOR = {Winkelmann, R. and Martin, M. A. and Haseloff, M. and Albrecht, T.
               and Bueler, E. and Khroulev, C. and Levermann, A.},
      TITLE = {The {P}otsdam {P}arallel {I}ce {S}heet {M}odel ({PISM-PIK})
               {P}art 1: {M}odel description},
    JOURNAL = {The Cryosphere},
     VOLUME = {5},
       YEAR = {2011},
      PAGES = {715--726},
        DOI = {10.5194/tc-5-715-2011},
   }

If your study relies heavily on certain PISM sub-models (such as hydrology, calving,
fracture mechanics, thermodynamics) please contact us for information on additional
citations.

Citing the PISM project
%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%

You can cite all PISM versions by using the DOI `10.5281/zenodo.1199019
<https://doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.1199019>`_. This DOI represents all versions and will
always resolve to the latest one. Please see :numref:`tab-pism-doi` to download the
corresponding BibTeX record.
